About Me

I'm an ECE Undergraduate at Arunai Engineering College (2025–29), based in Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu. My focus is on VLSI & Digital Design, and my journey into engineering started with curiosity about how circuits power the devices we use every day.

I've been actively learning programming languages like C and Java, alongside hardware description languages such as Verilog HDL. This combination of software and hardware skills allows me to approach problems from multiple perspectives.

My goal is to specialize in building embedded systems and eventually contribute to chip design and FPGA development. I believe that understanding both the software and hardware layers is essential for creating efficient, real-world solutions.
